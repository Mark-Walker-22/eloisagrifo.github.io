Eloísa Grifo



I am a commutative algebraist at the Department of Mathematics
at the University of California, Riverside,
where I will be an Assistant Professor starting in the Fall of 2020.

During the academic year of 2019—2020, I am a visiting postdoc at the University of Utah, although UCR is my home base.

Previously, I was a postdoc at The University of Michigan.
My advisor was Craig Huneke, and I got my PhD in the University of Virginia.
Before that, I studied at Instituto Superior Técnico, in Portugal, where I first learned commutative algebra from Maria Vaz Pinto.
